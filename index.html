Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines eggs, cheese, and your choice of fillings.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2â€“3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, veggies, meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the pan: Heat butter or oil in a non-stick skillet.
  3. Cook: Pour the eggs into the pan. Cook over medium heat.
  4. Add fillings: When almost set, add your desired fillings.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette in half and slide onto a plate.
